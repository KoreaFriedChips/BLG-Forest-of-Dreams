about us

We are a day care facility licensed and designated by the government of New Brunswick, providing childcare services to children from 4 months to 12 years of age, following the New Brunswick Early Learning and Child Care Curriculum. Two owners have invested their world expertise in teaching and managing into this Centre. They hold degrees in Master of BA and Master of Early Learning and Childcare.

our services

We offer a half day preschool program that runs in the mornings from 8:30 am - 12:00 pm for preschool kids. 2 healthy snacks are provided to our full day children. Our childcare center is located in Saint John, just 2 minutes of drive from McAllister Place. The owners also extended the facility and newly purchased a forest that offers a beautiful, clean and safe environment, and fun experiences in nature. Our centre is uniquely designed for the kids to happily explore, learn, create, and grow.
